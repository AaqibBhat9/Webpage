About School The School Campus The School Campus is situated on the outskirts of Srinagar city, about 12 kms from city centre Lal Chowk, and 2 kms from Kashmir University campus, sprawled over 100 kanals of land.

Academics

Registered with CBSE, New Delhi, IDPS, Srinagar begins its academic session in the month of April and offers schooling from pre primary level through class X.

Beyond Classroom

Besides academics, students are encouraged to engage in a host of sports such as cricket, basketball, football, badminton, volleyball, swimming, Judo, yoga, Kho Kho etc.
